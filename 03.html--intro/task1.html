Homemade Apple Pie Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 6-8 medium sized apples
  • 1 cup granulated suger
  • 2tablespoons all-purpose
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoon butter
  • 2 unbaked pie crusts

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 220C
  2. In a large mixing bowl
  3. Place one of the pie crusts into a 9 inch pie dish
  4. Pour the apple mixture into the pie crust
  5. Dot the top of the apple mixture with small pieces of butter
  6. Place the second pie crust over the apples and crimp the edges to seal the pie
  7. Using a sharp knife, make a few small slits in the top crust to allow steam to escape
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 45-50 minutes
  9. Remove the pie from the oven and let it cool before serving

Serving Suggestion:

Serve your homemade apple pie warm with a scoop of vanilla ice cream for a delicios treat!

Enjoy!
